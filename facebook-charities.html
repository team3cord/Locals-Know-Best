$250 million for your communities in Oregon.

Our merger with Washington Federal will bring over $250 million in community development funding during the next five years to Oregon and over $100,000 in direct contributions to our local charities in 2013 alone. Click on the charity logo or “Like” your choice from the list below and you’ll be taken to Facebook where you can select Like and we’ll make a $5 donation or grant to that organization. Be sure to let your friends know how they can benefit their favorite charitable organization by voting too.
